Returns & Refunds Policy

At SutiYam, we take pride in the quality and detail of every item we sell. As a boutique brand, our goal is to ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining fairness and transparency.

❌ No Returns or Refunds (Unless Faulty)

  • We do not accept returns or issue refunds for reasons such as change of mind, incorrect size, or personal preference.
  • If the item you receive is faulty, damaged, or not as described, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • In such cases, we will arrange an exchange or refund after assessing the issue.
  • Proof of fault (such as a photo or video) may be required.

If you’re unsure about sizing, style, or fit, feel free to contact us before placing your order — we’re happy to assist!
